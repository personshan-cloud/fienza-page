Fienza FAQs

Where are Fienza products made?+
Fienza products are primarily designed in Australia for Australian homes and compliance standards. All products meet WELS and WaterMark requirements for use in Australia and New Zealand.
What warranty do Fienza products come with?+
Fienza ceramic products carry up to a 15-year structural warranty. Tapware mechanisms are covered for 10 years, and cabinetry comes with a manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship.
Are Fienza toilets WELS certified?+
Yes. All Fienza toilet suites sold at MyHomeware carry a WELS 3 or 4-star water efficiency rating. Dual flush systems meet or exceed Australian regulatory requirements.
What materials are Fienza vanities made from?+
Fienza vanity cabinets are constructed from E0 MDF board and FSC-certified timber panels. Moisture-resistant construction makes them suitable for humid bathroom environments. Tops are available in ceramic, stone, solid surface, and 304-grade stainless steel.
Can I match Fienza tapware finishes across different products?+
Yes — Fienza tapware, accessories, and toilet flush buttons share consistent finish codes across Gloss White, Matt Black, Brushed Gold, Brushed Nickel, Chrome, Gunmetal Grey, and Brushed Bronze.
How do I find the right Fienza toilet for my bathroom?+
Start by measuring your set-out distance to determine S-trap or P-trap. Then choose between back-to-wall, close-coupled, wall-hung, or in-wall cistern styles. Use Live Chat or book an in-store appointment for personalised advice.

Why Fienza?

When it comes to outfitting an Australian bathroom, Fienza bathroom products offer a rare combination of refined design and proven durability. Every Fienza vanity, Fienza toilet suite, Fienza tapware set, and Fienza basin is engineered to meet the specific demands of Australian homes — from humid coastal environments to hard-water inland regions.

Fienza tapware and accessories are available in a coordinated suite of finishes — including Matt Black, Brushed Gold, Brushed Nickel, Chrome, and Gunmetal Grey — so every element of your bathroom works together seamlessly. Whether you're designing a sleek contemporary ensuite or a warm Hamptons-style family bathroom, Fienza's diverse collections deliver the pieces to realise your vision without compromise.

Sustainability is central to how Fienza products are made. All Fienza toilets carry WELS 4-star water efficiency ratings. Fienza vanity cabinets use E0 MDF and FSC-certified boards — chosen for both environmental responsibility and long-term moisture resistance.
